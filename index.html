Scott Wills

Art Director - Production Designer

About

Scott Wills is an artist/illustrator who has been in the animation industry since 1990. He is most known for his works as a background illustrator for Samurai Jack and Primal, along with being a production designer for Hotel Transylvania 3.

Some of the other works he has done as an illustrator is helping with the art direction of Flushed Away, The Road to El Dorado, and The Clone Wars cartoon.
